# Tactile by Vellium

Transform your keyboard into a premium mechanical experience with satisfying sound profiles that boost your focus and typing rhythm.

## Rediscover Your Typing Experience
Tactile adds acoustic depth to your workspace, turning every keystroke into a satisfying experience. Enhance your focus and find your flow with our rich library of mechanical switch sounds.

## Seamless Customization
Beyond our extensive builtin library (Linear, Clicky, Tactile), you can effortlessly integrate your own custom sounds via drag-and-drop. Designed for performance, Tactile operates with minimal system impact and features a minimalist, dark-themed UI that feels like a native part of Windows.

## Key Features

- **Diverse Sound Library:** A wide range of professional mechanical switch and material profiles.
- **Custom Audio Support:** Easily add your own sound files in seconds.
- **Smart Filtering:** Quickly navigate and search through hundreds of profiles.
- **Optimized Performance:** Engineered for zero-delay and low CPU usage.
- **Native Design:** Sophisticated, Windows 11-inspired aesthetics with dark mode.
